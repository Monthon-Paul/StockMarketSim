Overview:

This Applicaiton is to replicate the Stock Market Simulator, it's to have the basis idea of how the Stock Market works. Users can intend to create a Portfolio to keep track of the Stocks, they can search and see how a company stock is handling. The intend is it to be a small application where the User can Buy/Sell stocks to make a profit. The User can save their process into a ".stk", example being "stock.stk".
